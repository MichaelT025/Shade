Restore Defaults?

This will delete all custom modes and reset default modes to their original settings. This action cannot be undone.

Reset Prompt?

This will reset the system prompt. Default modes revert to their factory prompt; custom modes will be cleared. Your provider and model selections will not be changed.

Delete All Data?

This will permanently delete ALL conversations and screenshots. This action cannot be undone.
